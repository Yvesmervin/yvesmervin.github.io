DESCRIPTION
Cut on the bias for a fluid, body-skimming fit, this highliy adjustable dress is made from an upcycled silk chiffon sari.
Slits along the sides are fastened with delicate chains and clasps, allowing you to reveal more or less, depending on your mood.
Toggles on the shoulders slide along a chain extension that allows you to lower or raise the neckline and even create assymetrical shapes.
Each piece is made to order from a curated catalogue of available sari fabrics so you can chose the one that speaks to your personal style.
Because every sari is unique, each dress is a one-of-a-kind piece with its own character and history.
It's an invitation to be part of the design process— and to invest in something truly yours.
