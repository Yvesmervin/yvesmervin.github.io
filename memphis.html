Memphis Coat
 A degrade of burgundy ranch mink is mounted on silk organza in this modern take on a fur coat.
A broadtail belt as well as broadtail straps on the cuffs allow for easy customization of fit.
Acrylic and Fur
 A laser-cut acrylic collar is juxtaposed with the classic aesthetic of mink. A combination of machining and hand tooling is used to shape the epaulets which are attached with custom mink covered buttons.
Small squares of acrylic squares throughout the body tie the contrasting materials together.
