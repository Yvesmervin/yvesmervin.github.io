About The Designer
Yves is a New York City native independent designer.
He was homeschooled by his parents, a French designer and a Canadian artist, and learned to sew at a young age. This unorthodox upbringing influenced his approach to design. While attending the Fashion Institute of Technology, he experimented with cutting edge fabrication processes and unconventional materials to develop unique textiles.
During this time he developed a strong sense of personal style.
Timeless silhouettes are rendered with an architectural approach to construction. And fine fabrics are made to conform to the body, accentuating the unique beauty of the wearer.
